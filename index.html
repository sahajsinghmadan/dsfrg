About Kochi Metro Rail Corporation

Kochi Metro Rail Corporation Limited (KMRL) was established in 2011 as a joint venture between the Government of Kerala and the Government of India. As Kerala's first metro rail system, we began commercial operations in 2017, revolutionizing urban transportation in the Kochi metropolitan area with our commitment to sustainable, efficient, and accessible public transit.

Our Mission

To provide world-class, sustainable urban transportation solutions that enhance the quality of life for our passengers while promoting environmental conservation and economic development in the region. We are committed to delivering safe, reliable, and customer-centric services that set new standards in public transportation.

Key Services

  • Feeder Bus Services & Integration

Service Coverage

Our comprehensive 25.6-kilometer network connects major residential, commercial, and educational hubs across Kochi, spanning from Aluva in the north to Tripunithura in the south. The system serves as a vital transportation backbone, linking key areas including Ernakulam, Edappally, and Kakkanad, while providing seamless connectivity to important destinations such as the High Court, Medical College, and various IT parks.
